Product Information
Overview
Product Name: Tiệm In Nhanh (Photobox Việt Nam)

Website: https://tieminnhanh.com

One-liner: A self-service photo printing kiosk that lets you pick photos from your phone and print them instantly on-site.

What It Does
Users scan a QR code at a physical kiosk, select photos from their phone gallery, arrange them into a layout using a drag-and-drop tool, and receive a printed A4 page within seconds. The service supports single photos, collages, photo strips, and ID or mini-size prints. No app download or waiting in line is required.

Key Features

Collage layout: drag-and-drop tool to arrange multiple photos on one page
Photo strip: classic film-style strip layout for a retro look
ID and mini photos: quick printing of passport-style or small-format photos
QR code workflow: scan a code at the kiosk to connect your phone instantly
No app required: entirely browser-based, no installation needed
